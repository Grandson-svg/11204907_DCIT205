CYBER SECURITY

Cybersecurity is a branch of computer science that focuses on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, damage, or theft. It involves the development and implementation of security measures to prevent and detect potential threats, such as hacking, malware, and data breaches. Cybersecurity professionals work to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information and systems, and to mitigate risks and vulnerabilities in order to maintain a secure computing environment.
In addition to protecting computer systems and data, cybersecurity also involves investigating and responding to security incidents, conducting risk assessments, and developing policies and procedures to ensure the ongoing security of an organization's information assets. It also includes areas such as cryptography, secure software development, network security, and incident response. As technology continues to advance, cybersecurity becomes increasingly important in safeguarding our digital world.
